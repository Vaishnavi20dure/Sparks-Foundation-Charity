"Sparks Foundation Charity is a big family
and work worldwide"

We work for the children in need of care and protection are with the following categories - Orphans, abandoned children, poor children, children separated due to migration or armed conflict, missing or run-away children, street children, abused, tortured and exploited children, children indulging in substance abuse, Children affected by natural calamities, emergencies and manmade disasters, children with disabilities.

The key aspects of our foundation are trustworthiness, honesty, and openness to every person who has invested time, money, and faith into the foundation. Support us so we can make better world for them together!!!
